# BIO Sculpture Brand Guidelines

This document outlines the brand guidelines for BIO Sculpture, ensuring consistent brand expression across all touchpoints.

## Brand Identity

**Brand Voice:** Professional, inspiring, ethical, and passionate  
**Brand Look:** Clean and contemporary, offering a curated color palette, allowing photography to shine  
**Core Values:** Healthy | Ethical | Professional

## Brand Colors

BIO Sculpture's brand colors were chosen to reflect our commitment to healthy, ethical, and professional nail care. The white evokes a sense of purity and cleanliness, while the champagne and pink add a touch of warmth and femininity. These colors are all neutral and calming, which is in line with our mission to provide our customers with a relaxing and enjoyable nail care experience.

### Primary Colors

#### WHITE
- **RGB:** 255, 255, 255
- **CMYK:** 0, 0, 0, 0
- **HEX:** #FFFFFF
- **Usage:** Primary background color, represents purity and cleanliness

#### BLACK
- **RGB:** 0, 0, 0
- **CMYK:** 70, 50, 30, 100
- **HEX:** #000000
- **Usage:** Primary text color, provides strong contrast

#### CHAMPAGNE (Pantone 8003c)
- **RGB:** 135, 125, 113
- **CMYK:** 37, 37, 42, 26
- **HEX:** #857D71
- **Usage:** Accent color, adds warmth and sophistication

#### SWEET BIANCA (Pantone 705c)
- **RGB:** 238, 217, 222
- **CMYK:** 2, 18, 6, 0
- **HEX:** #EED9DE
- **Usage:** Accent color, adds femininity and softness

### Color Usage Guidelines

- **Strong contrast** between dark text and light background enhances legibility, especially for lengthy content or for users with visual impairments
- While darker colors can make a statement, use them **sparingly** to avoid overwhelming your audience
- Consider the overall color scheme and maintain a **harmonious balance** between light and dark elements
- Use light backgrounds (white, Sweet Bianca) for primary content areas
- Use medium backgrounds (Champagne) sparingly for emphasis
- Use dark backgrounds (Black) sparingly for dramatic effect

## Typography

### Primary Font Family

**Jost** (Google Fonts) - Alternative to Futura for web applications

- **Headings:** Jost Medium (500) - equivalent to Futura Md BT (Medium)
- **Body Text:** Jost Light (300) - equivalent to Futura Lt BT (Light)
- **Regular Text:** Jost Regular (400)

### Font Fallbacks

For internal documents (emails, Excel, Word files) where recipients might not have the primary font installed:

1. **Tahoma (Bold)** - Pre-installed on Windows PCs
2. **Calibri Light** - Pre-installed on Windows PCs
3. **Jost** - Available through Google Fonts

### Font Case Guidelines

#### Title Case
Brand, Sub-Brands, and individual product names should use **Title Case**. This means capitalizing the first letter of each word, with exceptions for minor words like "of" and "the."

**Examples:**
- Potpourri Aqua Soak
- Lavender Base
- Professional Career Training
- Apex Gel
- Contour Base & Build

**Note:** This also applies to the names of the colors.

#### Brand Names (Title Case)
- Bio Sculpture
- Biogel
- Evo
- Bi-olygel
- Ethos
- Gemini
- Spa

## Brand Benefits

A unified brand voice extends far beyond aesthetics. A cohesive brand image:

1. **Fosters Trust and Recognition** - When customers encounter Bio Sculpture across different touchpoints, they're greeted by a familiar and consistent narrative, building trust and solidifying brand recognition.

2. **Amplifies Messaging** - A unified voice ensures clarity and impact, ensuring the core values of Healthy | Ethical | Professional reach their audience powerfully and effortlessly.

3. **Strengthens Community** - Consistent brand expression fosters a sense of belonging and shared purpose within the Bio Sculpture brand, uniting stakeholders in shared goals.

4. **Elevates Professionalism** - A polished and unified brand image exudes professionalism, reflecting positively on all who represent Bio Sculpture.

## Design Principles

1. **Clean and Contemporary** - Minimalist design that allows content to shine
2. **Photography First** - Let high-quality product and service photography take center stage
3. **Neutral Palette** - Use brand colors to create calming, professional environments
4. **Consistent Voice** - Maintain professional, inspiring, ethical, and passionate tone across all communications
5. **Quality Focus** - Emphasize health, sustainability, and quality in all messaging
